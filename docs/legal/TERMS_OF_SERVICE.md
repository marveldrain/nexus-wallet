## 1. What the App is

Nexus is a **non-custodial** software wallet that lets you generate,
store (encrypted, on your own device), and use cryptographic keys to interact
with public blockchains (currently Bitcoin, Ethereum and other EVM-compatible
networks, and Solana). We do not custody funds, do not operate an exchange,
and are not a party to any transaction you make using the App — the App is a
tool you use to interact directly with public blockchain networks and
third-party infrastructure (see the Privacy Policy for the list).
