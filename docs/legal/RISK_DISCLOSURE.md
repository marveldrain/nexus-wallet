Before using Nexus Wallet ("**Nexus**", "**the App**", "**we**"), read and
understand the following. By using the App you acknowledge each of these
risks.

## 1. Nexus is non-custodial

Nexus never has access to your recovery phrase, private keys, or funds. Your
recovery phrase and private keys are generated on your own device and never
transmitted to us or anyone else. We cannot see your balances beyond what's
publicly visible on the blockchain, cannot freeze or reverse a transaction,
and cannot recover your funds under any circumstances — including if you lose
your recovery phrase, forget your password, or send funds to the wrong
address.

**There is no "forgot password" flow for your recovery phrase.** If you lose
both your device password and your written-down recovery phrase, your funds
are permanently and irrecoverably lost. No one — not us, not a support team,
not a court order — can reverse this.

## 2. You are solely responsible for your recovery phrase

Anyone who obtains your recovery phrase (the 12 or 24 words shown during
setup) can take everything in your wallet, instantly and irreversibly. We
strongly recommend:
- Writing it down on paper (not a screenshot, not a password manager, not a
  cloud note, not an email to yourself) and storing it somewhere only you
  can access.
- Never entering it into any website, app, or message — we (and no
  legitimate service) will ever ask for it.
- Treating anyone who asks for your recovery phrase as an attempted theft.

## 3. Blockchain transactions are irreversible

Once a transaction is broadcast and confirmed, it cannot be cancelled,
reversed, or refunded — by us or anyone else. Sending funds to the wrong
address, the wrong network, or an incompatible asset type typically results
in permanent loss. Double-check every recipient address and network before
confirming a send.

## 4. Network and smart contract risk

Nexus relies on third-party blockchain networks (Bitcoin, Ethereum and other
EVM chains, Solana) and third-party infrastructure (RPC providers, price
feeds, block explorers) that we do not control or operate. These networks and
the contracts deployed on them can have bugs, be congested, fork, or change
in ways outside our control. Custom tokens you add are smart contracts
written by third parties we have not audited — adding a token does not imply
we vouch for it.

## 5. Market risk

Cryptocurrency and token prices are highly volatile and can lose most or all
of their value. Past performance shown in any chart is not indicative of
future results. Nothing in the App is financial, investment, or tax advice.
